Biography

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the School of Nursing at the University of British Columbia (UBC). My doctoral research explored the role of central sensitization and somatic activating KRAS mutations in endometriosis. Currently, my postdoctoral research is exploring knowledge needs on chronic pelvic pain of health care providers and developing an online resource about chronic pelvic pain addressing these needs. My broad research interests include endometriosis, chronic pelvic pain, central sensitization, knowledge translation, and medical education. For more information about my research, check out my publications page!
